About Me

I am a PhD Student at the Embedded Systems Engineering (ESE) section of the DTU Compute at the Technical University of Denmark. I do research in predictive maintenance using tinyML. I am happy to supervise any BSc or MSc student interested in working with tinyML. See the Student Projects part of this website for more information. I started my PhD studies on the 1st of September 2021, and I expect to finish on the 31st of August 2024. My supervisors are Xenofon Fafoutis as main supervisor and Jan Madsen as co-supervisor. I am a Teaching Assistant at the Operating Systems and Systems Optimization courses.
