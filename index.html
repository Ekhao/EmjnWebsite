About Me

I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the Embedded Systems Engineering (ESE) section of the DTU Compute at the Technical University of Denmark. I do research in predictive maintenance using tinyML. I did my PhD studies at the same institution from the 1st of September 2021 to the 31st of December 2024.
